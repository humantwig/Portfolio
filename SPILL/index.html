The Installation

This installation will be avaliable from the 6th to the 23rd of April at Te Komititanga Square from 10am till 6pm daily.

What is Spill?

Spill is an interactive storytelling installation that acts as a place where people can take some time, and take on the role of a character, learning and engaging in conversation about common everyday financial scenarios that every young adult goes through, also offering tips and tricks that could help build a healthy financial future.

The Design

By “entering” under the cylindrical installation, visitors can create their own micro space where they could briefly escape and take in the full-on interactive experience in private. It creates a unique way to discuss and engage with financial problems in the most intimate way.
